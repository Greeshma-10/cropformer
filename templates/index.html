The Technology Behind Our Selector

Our platform is powered by an interpretable deep learning framework designed specifically for genomic prediction. The model architecture combines two powerful components:

  • Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) to effectively extract features and patterns from raw SNP data.
  • Multi-Head Self-Attention, the core mechanism of Transformers, to capture complex, long-range interactions between different genetic markers across the genome.

This hybrid approach allows our model to achieve state-of-the-art accuracy, identifying the subtle genetic signals that influence key agricultural traits.
